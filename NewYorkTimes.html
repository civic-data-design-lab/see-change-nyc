NEW YORK CITY COUNCIL IN THE NEWS
Text Analysis of the New York Times Article from 2013 -2020

To analyze topics covered in New York Times articles, topic modeling was performed on a set of articles from the New York Times. Using Gensim and NMF (a machine learning algorithm for topic modeling), key topics were extracted from articles covering New York City Council for each year from 2013 through 2020. Next, articles were tagged according to topics. Using tagging, related topics were identified (e.g. “policing” and “cornavirus“ topic are covered in the same articles)
