NYC Council in the News
Using the New York Times API, all articles which mentioned the New Your City Council were downloaded since the de Blasio Mayoral election (January 2013- Summer 2020). These 2,510 articles were then run through a machine learning topics modeler to identify the topics of the articles. Using that data this visualization allows us to understand the biggest issues City Council has faced over the last seven years from the Foie Gras Ban to Education.
