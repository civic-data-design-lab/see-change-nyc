What issues are NYC city council members talking about?

New York City Council in the News

Using The New York Times API, we downloaded all articles that mentioned the New York City Council since the de Blasio mayoral election (Winter 2013-Summer 2020). 2,510 articles were then run through a machine learning topics modeler to identify the topics of the articles. Using that data, this visualization allows us to understand the issues the City Council has faced over the past seven years, from the foie gras ban to education.

Read more about data description and process

Data Limitations

It is possible that some articles were missed during The New York Times API search because an article might have been related to an issue on the City Council’s agenda but did not mention “city council”. The machine learning algorithm uses all the text from all the articles to create topics; this means that there is no indicator of what article directly contributed to a topic being identified. Therefore the relationship between articles and topics is relative.
