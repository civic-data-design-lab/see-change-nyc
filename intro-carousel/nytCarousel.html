We scraped all the New York Times articles that mentioned the New York City Council from January 2013 to July 2020.

A machine learning topic model was applied to the data to identify the most common issues that the City Council works on.

Education was the most discussed topic in the City Council, with more than 210 articles in the New York Times.

The data shows that the City Council was heavily involved in the matter of closing the Rikers Island jail.

In 2019, the City Council approved a $8 billion plan to close the jail complex and replace it with smaller borough-based jails across New York City by 2026.
