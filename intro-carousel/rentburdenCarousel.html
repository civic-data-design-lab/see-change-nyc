Over 45% of New York City households are rent burdened.

Residents of the Bronx are more likely to get priced out of their neighborhoods than New Yorkers in any other borough.

According to a 2018 study from the Regional Plan Association, households in 71% of the Bronx's census tracts are at risk of displacement.

In Bedford Park, 90% of households are considered rent burdened.

Moreover, rent burden disproportionately impacts communities of color. Bedford Park's population is 64.8% Hispanic.
