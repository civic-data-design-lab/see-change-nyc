What seats are open for the 2021 election?

2021 Open Seats

In a 2010 referendum, New York City voted to reinstate a two-term limit for City Council members. However, incumbent City Council members who were elected to office on or before November 2, 2010 could complete their current term and go on to serve up to two additional four-year terms. During the 2021 election, more than a decade since the two-term limit was reinstated, New York City will see an unprecedented 35 open positions. Add to that four borough presidents, the comptroller, and the mayor, and 2021 will be an election year that transforms the City. Use this visualization to explore the positions open for reelection.
